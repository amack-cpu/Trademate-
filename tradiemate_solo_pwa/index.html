A note from us
TradieMate helps you work smarter — but it's still your business, your signature, your call.

The AI learns from your data. The more you give it, the better it gets. But always cast an eye over quotes and invoices before they go out — especially early on.

We're not registered tax agents. The BAS summaries and calculations are a solid guide, not formal advice. Run them past your accountant at tax time if you're unsure.

Location and job data is stored securely to power your history and estimates. It stays yours. We don't sell it.

TradieMate AI is a very capable apprentice — not a licensed professional. Check its work.

By using TradieMate, you acknowledge that quote accuracy improves over time and is based on your historical data. Direct ATO lodgement is made on your behalf — you remain responsible for the accuracy of the submission.
